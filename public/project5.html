IntelliStep - Smart Stick for the Blind

A smart assistive device designed to enhance mobility and safety for the visually impaired through obstacle detection and alerts.

Key Features:

  • Ultrasonic and ESP32- CAM obstacle detection
  • Vibration motor feedback
  • Buzzer alerts for close-range hazards

Technologies Used:

Arduino, ESP32-CAM, Ultrasonic Sensors, Buzzer, Vibration Motor, Embedded C
